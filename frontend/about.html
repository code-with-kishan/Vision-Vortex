About This Project

Vision Vortex is a B.Tech CSE mini-project built to solve a real problem faced by small farmers: keeping track of antimicrobial medicine usage in livestock. Overuse or wrong dosage of antimicrobials can lead to Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR), which is a serious concern for both animal and human health.

Most small farms in our area still use paper registers for this. We wanted to try building a simple digital alternative using the web development and database concepts we learned in our first year.
